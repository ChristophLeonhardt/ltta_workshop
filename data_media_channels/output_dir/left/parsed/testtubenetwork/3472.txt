June 7, 2015
Guerrero, Mexico
Protesters burn electoral ballots in attempt
to disrupt the country’s midterm elections.
Violence has plagued the elections, with several
candidates murdered by gangs.
Teachers sabotaged voting stations in protest
of the government’s proposed education reforms.
They’re also calling for the return of 43
students, who have been missing since September
2014.
Despite facing several scandals, President
Enrique Peña Nieto's party will retain control
in Congress.
